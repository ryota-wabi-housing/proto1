Buy-Side Needs (Sample)

  • Hotels: ¥2–20B. Tokyo 23 wards, Osaka, Kyoto, Fukuoka, Sapporo, Nagoya. Inbound-heavy locations, 50–150 keys; operator change welcome.
  • Residential (bulk/entire): ¥0.1–10B (often ¥0.5–2.5B). Tokyo 23 wards, Osaka, Fukuoka, Sapporo, Nagoya. Minpaku-convertible assets welcome.
  • Office / Retail: ¥0.5–5B (often ¥1–3B). Core 5 wards, Akihabara, Ikebukuro. Vacant/older buildings OK; hotel/resi conversion common.
  • Land: ¥0.1–10B+. Azabu, Shibuya, Chiyoda, Shinjuku; near major 23-ward stations; Osaka CBD. Efficient frontage/FAR preferred.
  • Logistics: ¥2–50B. Greater Tokyo / Osaka / Nagoya / Fukuoka logistics corridors. Stable CF focus; factory sites considered.
  • Houses / Condos: ¥0.05–5B. Shibuya / Azabu / Akasaka. Vacant/leased/OC OK; 1LDK+.
