Our Services

Residential Properties

Gain access to Japan’s most desirable homes—from modern luxury apartments in central Tokyo to traditional houses in scenic neighborhoods.

Through our deep relationships with developers and property owners, we offer early and exclusive access to top-tier listings often unavailable on public platforms. Whether you’re seeking a permanent residence, a vacation home, or an investment property, we tailor recommendations to your needs, lifestyle, and goals.

  • ✓ Personalized consultations based on your budget, preferences, and timeline
  • ✓ Curated viewings and property reports
  • ✓ Bilingual negotiation and support through to contract signing
  • ✓ Optional post-purchase services (interior, property management, etc.)

Investment Properties

Unlock access to high-potential properties across Japan—including boutique hotels, resort developments, serviced apartments, residential buildings, and office spaces.

Whether you’re looking for a stable income-generating asset, a long-term appreciation play, or a pathway to residency, we guide you through the full investment journey with market insight and hands-on support.

  • ✓ Sourcing of vetted, off-market and public listings across Japan
  • ✓ Investment consultation (yields, visa eligibility, regional strategy)
  • ✓ Support with due diligence, legal, and tax structuring
  • ✓ Coordination with trusted local partners (legal, renovation, management)

Visa and Relocation Support

Whether you’re moving to Japan or investing as a path to residency, our multilingual team provides personalized guidance to simplify the entire process.

We work closely with certified immigration specialists, legal partners, and relocation experts to help you build the right visa strategy, secure the necessary documents, and settle into life in Japan with confidence.

  • ✓ Consultation on property-linked visa pathways (e.g. Business Manager Visa)
  • ✓ Referrals to trusted immigration lawyers and administrative scriveners
  • ✓ Assistance with company setup, bank accounts, and address registration
  • ✓ Support with relocation logistics, schooling, and daily life setup
