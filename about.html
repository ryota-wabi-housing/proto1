About wabi housing

wabi housing aims to provide a refined real estate experience for those entering the Japanese market.

We work closely with individuals, family offices, private banks, and asset management firms to deliver high-quality advisory and brokerage services.

Through transparent and reliable communication, we manage complex documentation and transactions in Japan, reducing stress for clients relocating or investing in the country.
